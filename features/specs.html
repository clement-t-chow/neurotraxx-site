Technical Specs

Platform

Native desktop application. Currently supported on Apple Silicon (M-series) Macs only.

Imaging Support

Structural and diffusion-weighted MRI, including ultra-high-field (UHF) acquisitions.

Visualization

Synchronized 2D (axial, sagittal, coronal) and interactive 3D views powered by VTK.

Coordinate Systems & Frames

Built-in support for common stereotactic frames and coordinate conventions, with custom frame/fiducial submissions accepted.

Tractography

Reconstruction and visualization of white matter pathways from diffusion data.

Navigation

Point-of-interest marking and tracking for subcortical navigation, MER, and DBS workflows.
