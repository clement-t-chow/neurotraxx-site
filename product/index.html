Product Overview

NeuroTraxx is a native desktop application for exploring ultra-high-field (UHF) structural and diffusion MRI as living 3D maps of the brain's wiring. It combines synchronized 2D/3D visualization, tractography, and subcortical navigation tools in a single fast, local workflow — built to support MER and DBS navigation.

See How It Works for a step-by-step walkthrough, or explore the core features.

Use Cases

Subcortical Navigation

Mark and track points of interest across synchronized 2D and 3D views during exploration and planning.

MER & DBS Workflows

Correlate microelectrode recording tracks and DBS targets with structural and diffusion imaging.

Intraoperative Validation

Validate trajectories in real time by correlating MER recordings with structural and diffusion imaging.

Tractography Research

Reconstruct and explore white matter pathways from diffusion-weighted data for research and education.
