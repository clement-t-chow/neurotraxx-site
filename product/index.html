Product Overview

NeuroTraxx is a native desktop application for exploring ultra-high-field (UHF) structural and diffusion MRI as living 3D maps of the brain's wiring. It combines synchronized 2D/3D visualization, tractography, and subcortical navigation tools in a single fast, local workflow — built to support MER and DBS navigation.

See How It Works for a step-by-step walkthrough, or explore the core features.

Use Cases


Symptom Mapping

Relate clinical signs and stimulation effects to anatomical targets and surrounding structures in synchronized 2D and 3D.

VTA Visualization

Visualize volumes of tissue activated (VTA) alongside structural and diffusion imaging to understand stimulation spread.

Tractography

Reconstruct and explore white matter pathways from diffusion-weighted data for research and education.

Subcortical Mapping

Mark and track points of interest across subcortical structures in synchronized 2D and 3D views.

Eloquent White Matter Tracts

Visualize critical white matter pathways to help plan trajectories that steer clear of eloquent tracts.

Trajectory Automation

Streamline repetitive trajectory and frame calculations so you can focus on anatomy and targeting.
