Frequently Asked Questions

Is NeuroTraxx FDA approved or cleared for clinical use?
No. NeuroTraxx is an investigational research tool intended for research, education, and exploratory use only. It is not FDA approved or cleared.

What platforms does NeuroTraxx support?
NeuroTraxx is currently supported on Apple Silicon (M-series) Macs only.

What imaging data does NeuroTraxx work with?
Structural and diffusion-weighted MRI, including ultra-high-field (UHF) acquisitions.

Can I use my own stereotactic frame or fiducials?
Yes — submit your custom frame or fiducial configuration on the Support page for review.

When will NeuroTraxx be available to download?
NeuroTraxx is moving toward a subscription-based licensing model. Contact sales@neurotraxx.com for early access.
