What NeuroTraxx Can Do

Tractography, Unlocked

Turn diffusion-weighted scans into reconstructed white matter pathways — see the brain's connections come to life.

3D That Actually Moves

Spin, slice, and explore a fully interactive 3D brain alongside synchronized axial, sagittal, and coronal views.

Built-In Frame & Coordinate Tools

Switch between coordinate systems and stereotactic frames on the fly, with everything kept in sync.

Subcortical Navigation

Mark points of interest and trace paths through subcortical structures, viewed from every angle at once.

MER & DBS Ready

Navigate microelectrode recording (MER) tracks and deep brain stimulation (DBS) targets with synchronized anatomical context.

Runs on Your Machine

A fast, native desktop app built with PyQt5 and VTK — no cloud, no waiting.

How It Works

  1. Bring your scans — Import imaging data, including diffusion-weighted sequences.
  2. Map the pathways — Reconstruct white matter tracts from diffusion data.
  3. Explore — Navigate anatomy and pathways together in synchronized 2D and 3D views.
  4. Save your work — Export points and views for later reference.
