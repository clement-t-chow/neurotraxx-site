How It Works

From raw imaging data to surgical guidance in four steps.

  1. Bring your scans — Import imaging data, including diffusion-weighted sequences.
  2. Map the pathways — Reconstruct white matter tracts from diffusion data.
  3. Explore — Navigate anatomy and pathways together in synchronized 2D and 3D views.
  4. Save your work — Export points and views for later reference.
